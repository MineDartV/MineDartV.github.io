About Us

Our Restaurant:

Here at aa Sushi, we have been creating Sushi in Washington since 2007. Our principal director Teresa Lee has allowed us to continue to make Sushi for years and years, we will be able to continue making Sushi. Anthony Christianto, one of our chefs, has been with us for a very long time. He is one of the main chefs and is who normally cooks our delicious Sushi. If you happen to stop by, make sure to say thank you for all his years of service making sushi. Since aa Sushi has been around for so long, we have learned a thing or two about making Sushi. We have improved on our sushi making and we make sure to learn from our mistakes and read our customer reviews. We hope to expand our restaurant to different places of Washington and eventually to other states to allow more people to have our Sushi. Thank you for supporting aa Sushi.
