AA SUSHI

Here at AA Sushi, we believe in treating our customers with the upmost respect. We hope to provide our customers with the best possible sushi for them. Sushi has been in America sine 1966 and we hope that we can give you the best possible Sushi. We have all types of different Sushi's such as Ikura, California Roll, Cucumber Roll, etc. If you would like to order some of our food, just go on over to our menu, then you can click online delivery or order delivery.
