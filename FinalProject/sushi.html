History of Sushi

This page is dedicated to the history of Sushi, without the people before us, we may have never created our restaurant, therefore we are grateful for them.

Sushi was first created in Southeast Asia around the 300-500 BCE. These original "Sushi" was raw salmon mixed with rice. This tradition would spread to China and eventually Japan around the 800 CE. Sushi was first introduced to America in the 20th Century. This is where Sushi would begin to be popularized within America. In the 1960's the first Sushi restaurants started popping up, from this, the food spread. People enjoyed the food as it tasted good and wasn't extremely hard to make. Some Sushi's became a delicate for people to eat. Sushi is eaten all across the world and is still made in Japan mainly but there are still many other countries that have Sushi.
