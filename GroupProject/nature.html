Here, on this page you will explore only a little bit of Washingtons Natural Beauty. Washington contains many mountains and even some volcanoes such as Mount St. Helens. On these mountains and volcanoes, many animals live such as coyotes, deer, rabbits, and more. The Natural Beauty of the lakes and the pacific ocean is quite a sight to see. When visiting Washington, we recommend that you visit the pacific ocean, atleast one of the lakes in Washington, and atleast one of the Mountains in Washington to see the 3 main natural beauties in Washington.

Animals

Washington is home to many beautiful animals. There are all sorts of animals such as: Coyotes,Bobcats,Raccoons,Black Bears, Birds, Wolves, Moose and much more! These animals have lived here for hundreds of years and have grown to live in the environment of Washington. Washington sometimes rains a lot, so these animals have adapted to live in this environment.

Nature

Overall, the beauty of Washingtons Nature is something that everyone should come see at least once. In Washington, you can do many different activities outdoors such as rock climbing, mountain biking, hiking, camping, and much more. My personal favorite is Mountain Biking. Overall, the Beauty of Washingtons Nature is something that people cannot miss out on. It is truly one of natures beauties.
