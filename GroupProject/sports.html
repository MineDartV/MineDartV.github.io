Seattle Sports

Washington Has many great sports teams for many different sports. The primary Washington sports teams are the Seattle Seahawks, The Seattle Sounders, The Kracken, And the Seattle Mariners. Besides professional sports, Washington also has a lot of different college-level sports, including two D1 schools. The University of Washington and Washington State University. The Washington sports culture is rich with excitement and crazy fans. Our stadiums are among the country's best and are often considered a bucket list item for sports fans.
