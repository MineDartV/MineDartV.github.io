There are many different ways that you can learn to mountain bike and as well as there are many different bikes to choose from when getting into Mountain Biking. You may want to start off with a hardtail which is a bike that does not contain a back suspension and only has the front suspension. Due to these bikes not having the back suspension, they are relatively cheaper than full suspension bikes. Full suspension bikes are mainly more expensive if you want to get a good quality bike. Sadly, if you wanted to take the bike that you got from a retail store it would not work out so well on the trails. They can be used for commuting but MTB is much rougher than a road.
