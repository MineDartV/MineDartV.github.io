I was able to create a pallette of colors from an image!

This was the image that inspired me to create this color pallette

I am using a complementary color scheme. The red primary color and the tertiary orange ish color are complementing the green as they are on opposite sides of the color wheel. I am trying to elicit the emotion of warmth within the person. Warmth is normally associated with reds and oranges, so I used those colors then I added a spark of green in order for it to stand out.
